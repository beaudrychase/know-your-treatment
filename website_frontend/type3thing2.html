Cipro (Ciprofloxacin)

Cipro is used to treat urinary tract infections like cholera.

Average Dosage

Adults are recommended to take 500 mg each day.

Side Effects

Swelling / tearing of a tendon, numbness, tingling, burning pain, tenderness, stiffness, movement problems, severe stomach pain, fast or pounding heartbeats, skin rash, confusion, tremors, muscle weakness, light-headedness, no urination, jaundice, increased pressure inside the skull, low blood sugar, nausea, vaginal itching, headache, abnormal liver

Ingredients

ACTIVE: ciprofloxacin

INACTIVE: crospovidone, hypromellose, magnesium stearate, polyethylene glycol, silica colloidal anhydrous, succinic acid, and titanium dioxide

Manufacturer

bayer
